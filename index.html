From Lost in Translation to Global Connection

The Challenge

Dr. Tanaka, a renowned AI researcher from Tokyo University, was invited to deliver a series of lectures across India. Despite her expertise, the language barrier made education inaccessible:

  • Most lectures were delivered through human interpreters, causing delays and inaccuracies
  • Technical terms often got mistranslated, confusing students
  • The emotional connection was lost in translation
  • Q&A sessions became impractical due to the slow back-and-forth

How Linguis AI Made the Difference
